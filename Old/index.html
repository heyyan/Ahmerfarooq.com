about

Ahmer Farooq. Born in 1980, Lahore, Pakistan. Lives and works in Lahore, Pakistan.

Artists Biography

I am an artist, entrepreneur, and marketeer based in Lahore. Over a number of years I have devoted my creative energies on art and theater, and have curated art shows, conducted research on art and worked as a consultant and event manager.

I work with mixed medium to produce contemporary art that depicts current concerns, anxieties, and aspirations by focusing on personalities and movements in an evocative and alluring manner.

My artwork has been showcased in various venues in the UK, The UAE and Pakistan. I have exhibited my work and curated shows at the Art Gallery, Alhamra Academy of Performing Arts in Lahore. My two solo exhibitions “The Secrets of Eve” and “Eve in all her hues” were well received and were sold-out.

Over the years I have developed a signature style using a colorful palette that has been recognized globally. I was awarded The Stephen Medd ‘Artist of the Year Award’ in 1999. My art is held in private collections in the UK, USA, Norway, Denmark, UAE, India and Pakistan.

I honed my artistic and creative skills through formal and semi-formal trainings. I completed my basic art education from Bedford Modern School and continued on to develop my broader professional skills with a MBA from Imperial College. Currently, I work for a high street fashion brand as Director Marketing in Lahore, Pakistan.
